About Samuel Jeremiah Zhokwo (Samjez)

Samuel Jeremiah Zhokwo, short for SAMJEZ, is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Samjez Computers And Cafe Limited.

He was born into the family of Mr. and Mrs. Samuel. He is the third born of the house and the first son of his family, born on 23rd July 2000 in Abuja.

He is from the Federal Capital Territory Abuja, a native of Abuja, and holds a first degree in Computer and Mathematics from the National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN).

He started his computer journey as a computer operator at Success Computer Business Center, Gwako Gwagwalada FCT Abuja in 2013, and earned a diploma in Computer Operations by 2014. He has been working on his dreams for over 11 years now.

He owns a computer Internet café in Bassa Jiwa Airport Abuja FCT, which has been legally operating since five years now (2022).

His passion for bringing technology to everyone’s doorstep led him to venture into laptop dealership, which he has been running for over three years now (2024). The laptop showroom will soon be ready.

He started this dream with no money, no connection, no help — just hope and the back of dreams.

“I hope if I can do it, you can also do it. If not Samjez Computers And Cafe, it can’t be like Samjez Computers And Cafe.”
